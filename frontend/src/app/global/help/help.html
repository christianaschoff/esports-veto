How does the veto process work

A veto process has (two) three major steps:

  1. Creation
  2. Veto
  3. Observing

Creation

Creating a new veto is simple: click on "New" in the topbar, select your game and then decide what kind of veto system you want to use.
To give you an example:

  • Pick the Game "StarCraft2".
  • On the next page you must decide, if it is a 1v1 up to a 4v4 game.
  • Then you must decide if it is a Best Of 1 up to a Best of 7.
  • Finally you must decide the veto system (ABBA or ABAB).

Congratulations! You provided all information needed to start a new veto process.
We strongly recommend to provide player names to enhance the user experience significantly.
By pressing "CREATE VETO PROCESS" a new process is startet.

Important

You will see four QR-Codes/links after creation.
These links must be used by the players.
Send them the QR-Code or link.
Do not lose them, since they can not be recovered!

Veto

The veto process is quite easy. Both players must join the session by using their link.
When both players are present, the veto process can begin.
Depending on the chosen veto system the players vote one after another until all maps are picked/baned.

Observing

All persons with the oberserver link can see the veto (-process). With this link you can also replay the veto process and integrate it into E-Sports coverage/streams etc.

There are multiple settings to tailor your observation experience to your needs.
You can access the settings via the symbol settings

There are a couple of shortcuts you can use via keyboard:

Control + f
Toggle between fullscreen and embedded mode
Control + d
Toggle veto information header
Arrow-left
Step back one map until no more maps are left
Arrow-Right
Step forward one map until no more maps are left
Arrow-Up
Clear the whole list of vetos
Arrow-Down
Show all maps
